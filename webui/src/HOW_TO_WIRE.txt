Interactive Eval Graph – How to wire

1) Replace your current `webui/src/EvalSparkline.tsx` with the one in this folder.
2) In `BoardPane.tsx`, update the sparkline render to pass `currentIndex`, `onClickIndex` and (optionally) `onHoverIndexChange`:

Example:
  <EvalSparkline
    series={evalSeries}
    height={96}
    currentIndex={Math.max(0, ply - 1)}
    onHoverIndexChange={(i) => {/* optional preview */}}
    onClickIndex={(i) => onRebuildTo(i + 1)}
  />

That’s it — hover shows a hairline + tooltip, and clicking a point jumps to that ply.
